    • Welcome!

    My name is Tim Lamberton. I am a researcher and programmer with a background in physics, maths and bioinformatics. I have developed and maintained open-source software for bioinformatics, and designed and implemented novel algorithms for clustering genomic sequence data. I designed and implemented an experimental programming language, which aims to be easy to learn and easy to use. I have developed website front- and back-ends, and standalone web applications. This website contains information on my past and present work, as well as programming-related writing, demos and other things. Have a look around!
